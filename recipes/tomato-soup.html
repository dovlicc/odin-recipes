Tomato Soup

Imagine a bowl brimming with warmth and vibrancy—a velvety sea of red, crowned with a swirl of cream. This is tomato soup at its finest. As you lift the spoon, the rich aroma of tomatoes, garlic, and herbs envelops you, promising a comforting journey for your taste buds. Each spoonful is a symphony of flavors—sweet, tangy tomatoes harmonizing with the earthiness of garlic and the fragrant dance of basil and oregano. The texture is pure silk, a result of careful blending that transforms humble ingredients into a luxurious experience. Whether you opt for the classic version or indulge in the creamy rendition, each spoonful brings solace and satisfaction, making tomato soup a timeless and comforting delight.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Carrot
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 celery stalk

Steps

  1. Saute vegetables
  2. Add tomatoes
  3. Season and simmer
  4. Blend
  5. Finish the cream
  6. Serve
